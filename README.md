# VN-Horror-Game

What the player does: The player is a survivor of the apocalypse suddenly put in charge of a group. Your job/ goal as the player is to keep your group alive with the fewest fatalities as possible without getting infected yourself. You will form bonds with the members of your group and have all the more reason to save them and yourself. 

What the goal is: Get to the rumored safe town where theyâ€™ve discovered a cure with as close to a full party as you can. Keep as many people alive as you can.

How the game ends: The game will have 2+ endings where you make it to the town or you go insane from the grief and guilt of losing your friends and teammates along the way.

Core Loop:
Get exposition through story
Make Choice
Deal with the consequences of that choice

Game loop:
You have a lot of options so it's basically every time you play you could get new dialogue or screens

Inspirations: 
Visual novel games like Doki Doki Literature Club
